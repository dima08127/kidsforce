Why do parents choose Kids' Force kindergarten?

Kids' Force is a combination of standards, modernity and creativity. We select the best of all traditional pedagogies, improve them and combine it with our own methodology of preschool education.

  • We creatively develop children in all directions. Art and singing are not the only areaswhere children show their creativity. Mathematics, English and reading classes are also accompanied by the use of non-standard thinking of children.

  • We use the author's method of child development . Our teaching method helps children to explore the world in a new way, to learn material through observation and interaction with the world around them.

  • Live streaming. Parents can see what their child is doing in the app at any time

  • Small groups. We form small groups to make it comfortable for the child and the teacher. Then each child will be fully covered with attention

Your cooperation and dedication to our kindergarten is valuable to us. Please share your questions and comments - we are always open to communication.

Groups of our kindergarten

  • English

    In our English classes, we organise events and quests. This technique helps to enhance the learning process. We have a physical activity break with English songs. Kids'Force is taughtby native speakers and qualified teachers who turn complex material into an adventurejourney to an unknown country.

  • Lego construction

    Lego construction is the development of fine motor skills, correct and quick orientation in space, development of attention, memory and creative thinking, group communication skills, dialogue, and expanding the understanding of the world around us. This is just a part of the possibilities offered by Lego classes.

  • Chess

    Chess was invented one and a half thousand years ago, but it is still relevant today. There is no alternative. As in the past, so today, chess develops creative, abstract, analytical and logical thinking. It is important for us that children are broadly developed, which is why this "eternal" game is in our arsenal of activities.
